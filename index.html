About Me

I’m Pranali, a passionate software developer with a love for transforming ideas into reality through code. My journey into the world of technology began at a young age, when I would spend countless hours tinkering with my first computer, captivated by the endless possibilities it held.

Fast forward to today, and that childhood curiosity has blossomed into a fulfilling career.
